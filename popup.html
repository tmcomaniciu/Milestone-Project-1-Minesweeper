How to Play Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper! The goal of the game is to uncover all cells on the grid without detonating any mines.

Instructions:

  • Left-click on a cell to reveal it.
  • Alt (Option) + Left-click to flag a cell as a mine.
  • Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell.
  • Avoid clicking on mines!

Good luck and enjoy the game!
